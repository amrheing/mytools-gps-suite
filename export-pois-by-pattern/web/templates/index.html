Pattern Examples

Wildcard Patterns

*-S28 POIs ending with "-S28"
Hotel* POIs starting with "Hotel"
*Bridge* POIs containing "Bridge"

Regular Expression Patterns

.*-S\d+$ POIs ending with "-S" + numbers
^! .+ POIs starting with "! "
(AVOID|DANGER) POIs containing warnings

? = matches exactly one character

Pattern matching is case-insensitive

Regex Examples:

.*-S\d+$ Matches POIs ending with "-S" followed by numbers
^! .+-S\d+$ Matches POIs starting with "! " and ending with "-S" + numbers
Bridge|Tunnel Matches POIs containing "Bridge" OR "Tunnel"
\b(AVOID|DANGER)\b Matches POIs containing exact words "AVOID" or "DANGER"

Regular expressions provide powerful pattern matching

Matching is case-insensitive by default

What are POIs?

Points of Interest (POIs) are waypoints in your GPX file that mark specific locations like:

  • Landmarks and attractions
  • Warnings and hazards
  • Services and facilities
  • Photo spots and viewpoints
  • Navigation waypoints
